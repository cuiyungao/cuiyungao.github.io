Biography

I'm now a Postdoc in NTU, working with Prof. Yang Liu. I used to work as a Postdoctoral Fellow in CUHK (The Chinese University of Hong Kong), working with Prof. Michael R. Lyu. I also received my PhD degree at CUHK, and graduated in end of 2018. Before coming to CUHK, I was one bachelor student of the Department of Communication Engineering of Shanghai University (SHU) in 2012~2014, under the supervision of Prof. Wanggen WAN. Before starting my engineering career, I joined Chien Wei-zang College (钱伟长学院) in 2010, an Honors College of SHU, and graduated from the College in 2012.
